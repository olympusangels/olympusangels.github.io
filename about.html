About

PASSION. EXPERIENCE. DILIGENCE.

Olympus Angels was created with the idea that the entrepreneurial spirit should never be silenced due to lack of funding. Founded in Salt Lake City, Utah, we are an entrepreneur development organization dedicated to accelerating the creation of innovative, entrepreneur-led, high-growth businesses that will strengthen and expand the future growth of the regions's economy. Our vision is to create a robust entrepreneurial eco-system that fosters entreprenuerial talent, drives inovation to solve market needs, aligns community resources and support, and ensures funding for products, entrepreneurs and businesses of promise.
