Services

Olympus Angels facilities a wide range of executive leadership initiatives designed to bolster and expand the region's entrepreneurial eco-system comprised of talent, ideas, support and funding. We seek to directly lead entrepreneurial initiatives that promote the creation of new innovations and products or significantly improve existing products addressing a wide range of market needs. We do this with the end objective of building businesses that will agressively impact the vitality of our economy by creating jobs and increasing individual wealth.

At Olympus Angels, we use our own resources to fill critical gaps needed for economic and entrepreneurial expansion. We serve as a catalyst in forming new partnerships, developing innovative solutions, and capturing emerging opportunities that solve our economic issues and ensure a sound foundation for our economy in the future.

At Olympus Angels, we seek entrepreneurs looking for collaborative, on-going partnerships. We are passionate about investing in market opportunity and growth potential. In addition to venture capital investments, Olympus Angels also provides proven business expertise .
